About Me

25y, autistic, writer, and now venturing into the world of DJing and Music Production.

I'm the author of the sci-fi book ULTRA QUALIA and the drama/horror INTRUSIVE THOUGHTS (under my name, Leonardo Sales Santos). But everywhere else, I go by TRACK_N10—a name inspired by Charli XCX's iconic "Track 10."

My influences span from 6arelyhuman, 2hollis, Shygirl, and SOPHIE to Sega Bodega, Kim Petras, Nils Frahm, and countless other genres that shape my sound.

Born in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, I now live in Porto, Portugal. With a degree in Marketing and two postgrads in Sales and Data Science, I finally decided to face my fears and dive headfirst into music—something I couldn't imagine life without.

Music also became a way to fund my medication for autism and its comorbidities. If nothing else, being on the spectrum has given me a uniquely heightened sensitivity to art.

Music today. Music now. Music forever.
